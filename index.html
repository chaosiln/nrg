Welcome

To the pages of Chaoslin Nodrog



In the depths of the ancient earth,
A power lies, awaiting rebirth,
The Earth-bender, one with the land,
Thrives with a connection that's grand.


With hands outstretched, they feel the pulse,
Of nature's beat, an intimate convulse,
They shape the rocks, the soil, the stones,
Harnessing power, where it's sown.


With every step, they leave their mark,
Creating beauty in light or dark,
Majestic sculptures rise from the ground,
As harmony and balance resound.


Through valleys deep and mountains high,
The Earth-bender's spirit starts to fly,
Their passion ignites, their heart aglow,
A force of nature they proudly show.


With every bend, they restore and mend,
Healing wounds that time may send,
Connected to Earth's primal core,
They embrace its strength forevermore.


The Earth-bender's touch leaves trails of awe,
As they dance with elements without a flaw,
Their connection is fierce, unbroken, true,
A guardian of nature, through and through.
